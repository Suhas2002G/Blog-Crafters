About Blog-Crafters

Welcome to Blog-Crafters, the world’s most customer-centric company, where we focus on providing our customers with a seamless online shopping experience. Founded in 1994, Blog-Crafters started as an online bookstore and has evolved into a global leader in e-commerce, cloud computing, digital streaming, and artificial intelligence.

Our Mission

Blog-Crafters’s mission is to be Earth's most customer-centric company. We strive to continually raise the bar of customer experience by providing more choices, better prices, and innovative technology.

What We Offer

From humble beginnings as a bookstore, Blog-Crafters now offers a wide range of products and services:

  • Shopping: A vast selection of products from everyday essentials to rare items, all in one place.
  • Blog-Crafters Web Services (AWS): Leading cloud computing solutions trusted by top companies worldwide.
  • Prime Membership: Access to exclusive deals, faster shipping, Prime Video, and much more.
  • Digital Products: Blog-Crafters Kindle, Audible audiobooks, and Alexa-enabled devices to enhance digital lifestyles.
  • Innovative Technology: From AI-driven recommendations to cashier-less stores (Blog-Crafters Go), we are committed to innovation.

Our Values

We believe in building our business on a foundation of trust, transparency, and a commitment to our customers and employees:

  • Customer Obsession: Everything we do starts with a focus on our customers.
  • Operational Excellence: Continuously improving to better serve our customers and reduce costs.
  • Long-Term Thinking: Investing in technology and sustainability to make a positive impact on future generations.
  • Innovation: Bringing bold ideas to life and pushing boundaries.

Thank you for choosing Blog-Crafters. We are here to make your shopping experience enjoyable, efficient, and secure.
